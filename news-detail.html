Husam Par Ban Kiun Laga?

Gully cricket ki duniya mein ek nayi musibat us waqt shuru hui jab Alijan ka mobile achanak ghaib ho gaya.

Is incident ke baad sabse zyada shak gaya ek teammate par — Husam.

Us din Husam ko aakhri baar Alijan ke paas dekha gaya tha, aur uske baad se uska rawayya kuch alag tha. Jab usse baat karne ki koshish ki gayi, to wo achanak door chala gaya. Ye sab us waqt hua jab mobile ghaib tha.

Zalmi ke captain Rafay ne turant decision liya: “Jab tak scene clear nahi hota, Husam match nahi khelega. Team se upar koi nahi hai.”

Alijan, jiska mobile chala gaya tha, ne kaha: “Agar kisi ne galti se bhi le liya hai to wapas de de. Main blame nahi kar raha.”

Ahmed — Alijan ka bhai aur Qalandars ke captain — ne bhi bayan diya: “Main kisi par ilzaam nahi laga raha, bas ye chahta hoon ke sach saamne aaye.”

Trust... gully cricket mein sirf runs ya wickets nahi, izzat bhi hoti hai. Ek mobile gaya, lekin team ka bharosa bhi tut gaya.

Ab sabki nazar hai is faislay par. Kya Husam wapas aayega? Kya mobile milega? Kya team ka trust wapas banega?

Ye sirf cricket nahi... ye hai gully ka faisla.
