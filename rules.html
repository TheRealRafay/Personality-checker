📋 Gully Cricket Rules

  • Ball kutton wale ghar mein gaya → jisne maara wo akela layega + -3 runs. Nahi laya to OUT.
  • Ball diwaar se lag ke catch → OUT.
  • Ball thamba ya darakht se lag ke catch → OUT.
  • Ball Anas ke ghar gaya → OUT.
  • Kisi aur ke ghar gaya → -3 runs.
  • Alijan ki chhat pe maara to → ball laana padega.
  • Ball Chachu Majid ke ghar mein gayi → dot ball.
  • Full game hogi, koi choti game nahi chalegi.
  • Koi kisi ki batting nahi le sakta.
  • Running dusre ke liye kar sakte ho.
  • Umpire ki baat final hai.
  • Cheating nahi chalegi, umpire bhi fair hoga.
  • Batting ki lalach nahi karni 😤
