📋 Gully Cricket Rules (By Rafay & Squad)

  • Agar ball kutton wale ghar mein gaya → jisne mara wo akela leke aayega aur -3 runs. Agar nahi laya → out.
  • Agar ball diwaar ko lag ke catch hua → out.
  • Agar ball thambe ya darakht ko lag ke catch hua → out.
  • Agar ball Anas ke ghar gaya → direct out.
  • Agar ball kisi aur ke ghar gaya → -3 runs.
  • Alijan ki chhat pe jo maarega → wo ball laayega.
  • Ball agar Chachu Majid ke ghar ke andar gayi → ball dot hoga (no runs).
  • Full game hogi — koi half match nahi chalega.
  • Koi kisi ki batting zabardasti nahi le sakta.
  • Doosron ki running allowed hai.
  • Umpire ki baat final hogi — no argument.
  • Cheating bilkul mana hai, umpire bhi cheating nahi karega.
  • Batting ki lalach nahi karni😤
